# Welcome to Hamza Clinic

Hamza Clinic is a premier dental care facility dedicated to providing top-quality oral healthcare services in a comfortable and friendly environment. Our team of experienced professionals ensures that every patient leaves with a healthy, confident smile.

## Operating Hours
- Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM - 7:00 PM
- Saturday: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
- Sunday: Closed
- Emergency Services: Available 24/7 (Please call our emergency hotline)

## Services Offered
At Hamza Clinic, we offer a comprehensive range of dental services:

1. General Dentistry:
   - Comprehensive Dental Exams and X-Rays
   - Professional Teeth Cleaning (Scaling and Polishing)
   - Fillings (Composite and Amalgam)
   - Tooth Extractions

2. Cosmetic Dentistry:
   - Teeth Whitening (In-office and take-home kits)
   - Porcelain Veneers
   - Dental Bonding

3. Orthodontics:
   - Traditional Metal Braces
   - Clear Aligners (Invisalign)
   - Retainers

4. Restorative Dentistry:
   - Dental Implants
   - Crowns and Bridges
   - Dentures (Full and Partial)
   - Root Canal Therapy

5. Pediatric Dentistry:
   - Child-friendly dental checkups
   - Fluoride treatments and Dental Sealants
   - Early orthodontic evaluation

## Pricing and Insurance
- Consultation Fee: $50 (Waived if a procedure is booked on the same day)
- Routine Cleaning: Starting from $100
- Teeth Whitening: Starting from $250
We accept most major dental insurance plans. Please contact our front desk prior to your appointment to verify your coverage. We also offer flexible payment plans for major procedures.
